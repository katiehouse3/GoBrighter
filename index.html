Leadership Coaching

Focusing on your strengths is a proven way to develop your leadership skills and achieve your short and long-term goals. Knowing what makes you talented and unique will help you perform better, be more productive, and have greater job satisfaction. In our strengths-based coaching sessions, we will:

  • explore your top talents as identified in the CliftonStrengths assessment (Gallup),
  • understand how to build talents into strengths, and
  • plan for how you can use your strengths to achieve your goals.

Sessions are conducted in-person or online using video conferencing.

Teambuilding

Unlock the power of your team by increasing team cohesion and building shared understanding of skills and personality styles. Research by Gallup has shown that investing in strengths-based team development has many organizational benefits, including profits, customer engagement and employee engagement. Strengths-based teambuilding workshops focus on:

  • Understanding individual talents on the team using the CliftonStrengths assessment (Gallup), and
  • Applying collective talents and strengths to team goals and projects.

Teambuilding programs are conducted in-person and last from ½ day to two days.

Culture Surveys

Your business performance is directly linked to your organizational culture. The Denison Culture survey offers an opportunity to assess your current culture and plan for improvement. Specifically, you will:

  • Understand areas of cultural strength and weakness
  • Backup your culture change initiative with hard data
  • Measure your culture improvement over time
  • Strengthen your business performance
  • Enhance your ability to attract and retain talent

Culture surveys can be customized for your company and needs. They are conducted online, with follow up workshops to support culture development.

About
